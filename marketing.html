Marketing Strategy

Product

Here at stellar times we sell smart watches with a classic look. Our watches are visually appealing and come with various features. With various watch straps room for customization is endless. Our website is visually appealing and captures the eye of any consumer. We plan to start small and distribute island wide and then eventually worldwide.

Price

Stellar time watches are arguably the most affordable on the market. Due to the fact we use recycled watch parts in an attempt for a more sustainable future we have cut cost a substantial amount. However, the price of each watch does vary from watch to watch depending on size and features. Each watch comes with a fixed strap although you may purchase others from our large line of stylish straps.

Our watches will be distributed locally at our manufacturing plant. We will have a separate building in which customers can come and look at the various watches to make sure they have exactly what they are looking for. However, if you are overseas a watch can be ordered from our website and it will be shipped to the consumer wherever they are. Having a physical store gives customers a chance to experience our pronounced customer service and get exactly what they want.
